Fried Chicken Recipe

Fried chicken features pieces of chicken coated in seasoned batter or breadcrumbs, then deep-fried until golden and crackling with crispiness. Beneath the crunchy exterior lies steaming, tender meat that bursts with flavor. Whether enjoyed plain, dipped in spicy sauce, or paired with sides like coleslaw or fries, it’s a satisfying mix of textures and bold seasoning.
