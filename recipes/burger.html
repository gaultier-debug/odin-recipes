Burger Recipe

A burger is built around a juicy patty—often beef—grilled until the outside is seared while the inside stays tender. It’s nestled between two soft buns and layered with lettuce, tomatoes, onions, pickles, and sauces like ketchup or mayonnaise. Each bite combines the smoky richness of the meat with the crunch of fresh vegetables and the pillowy softness of the bread.
